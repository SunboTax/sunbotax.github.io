Academic profile

CV

I am a Ph.D. candidate in the School of Computer Science and Engineering at Beihang University, affiliated with the Beijing Advanced Innovation Center for Big Data and Brain Computing (BDBC) and the ACT lab research unit. My research focuses on time series analysis, including long-term forecasting, sequence generation, and anomaly detection, as well as graph computing, especially graph reachability queries. My recent work was published at KDD, IJCAI, and ICML. My goal is to develop robust and deployable learning systems for complex real-world data.
